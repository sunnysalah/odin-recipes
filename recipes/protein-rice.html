Protein Rice

Homepage

Description:

This is a recipe that I make almost every day. I like to take advantage of the multiple health benefits of brown and black rice by mixing them together with some bone broth for extra protein.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • 1/4 cup of black rice
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic

Steps:

  1. Pour 1 tablespoon of oil into a pot
  2. Wash and mix in 1/4 cup of black rice
  3. Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt and pepper
  4. Cook on low heat for 45 minutes or until rice is fully cooked
  5. In a seperate pot, pour 1 tablespoon of oil
  6. Dice and add 3 cloves of garlic
  7. Dice and add 1 red pepper and 1/2 of an onion
  8. Stir around on low heat until pepper and onion is well caramalzied
  9. Add cup of brown rice
  10. Add 2 cups of bone broth
  11. Cook on low heat for 45 minutes or until rice is fully cooked
  12. Mix brown and black rice together and plate with your favorite protein source
  13. Enjoy thoughoughly
