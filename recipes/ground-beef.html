Ground Beef

Description:

This is another one of my go to recipes when I want to make a good protein source. This recipe combines tender ground beef mixed with onion, lemon juice and spices to make an absolutely delicios meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of 93% lean ground beef
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/4 cup of lemon joice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth

Steps:

  1. Finely dice 1/2 of an onion
  2. Put onion in bowl and mix in 1 lb of ground beef
  3. Pour in 1 tablespoon of oil
  4. Pour in 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  5. Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt and pepper
  6. Add 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  7. Mix thoughroughly until everything is mixed evenly
  8. Add 1 tablespoon of oil to pan on medium heat
  9. When oil starts smoking, add in the ground beef mixture
  10. Once oil starts to dry up, add in 1/2 cup of beef broth
  11. Cook until beef broth is almost dried up
  12. Pair with a nutritious carb like my protein rice recipe
  13. Enjoy thoughroughly
