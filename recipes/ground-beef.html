Ground Beef

Description:

This is another one of my go to recipes when I want to make a good protein source. This recipe combines tender ground beef mixed with onion, lemon juice and spices to make an absolutely delicios meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of 93% lean ground beef
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/4 cup of lemon joice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
