Juicy Jumbo Chicken

Description:

This is my Juicy Jumbo Chicken recipe. This is one of my favorite recipes to make during the week. I'll usually eat this 3-6 times per week.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you are going to need:

  • 1 lb of chicken breast
  • 2 teaspoons of spicy mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of an oil of your chocie
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken breast into small pieces
  2. Put cut chicken into mixing bowl
  3. Add in mustard, oil, salt, pepper and chili flakes
  4. Mix thoughoughly until chicken is fully coated
  5. Marinate for 1 hour
  6. Put 1 tablespoon of oil into a pan
  7. Cook chicken until cooked fully though
  8. Enjoy thoughroughly
