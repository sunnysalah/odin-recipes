Welcome to Cooking with Salah

Our Mission:

Our mission is to design nurtious, cheap and easy to make meals for athletes to cook at home. These meals have the perfect balance of carbs, proteins and fats as well as multiple micronutrients to help you recover optimally. These meals are delicious and personally made by our head chef, Salah.

About us:

Head chef Salah Lazzouni is a life long surfer and athlete. From running track in college to surfing nearly twice a day, Salah is usually pretty beat up from his rough training schedule. Because of this, Salah decided to study nutrition in order to optimize his recovery. Enter in the meals we make. Cheap, nutritious and hand tested... or should we say mouth tested.
